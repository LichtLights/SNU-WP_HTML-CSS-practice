Bladedancer's Umbral Blades Build - Last Epoch

Skills:

  • Umbral Blades (Max Rank)
  • Shuriken (Max Rank)
  • Decoy (Max Rank)
  • Smoke Bomb (Max Rank)
  • Shift (Max Rank)

Passives:

  • Shadow Dance
  • Deadly Precision
  • Rhythm of War
  • Blindspot
  • Evasion Mastery

Gear:

  1. Weapon: One-Handed Sword or Dagger with Increased Physical Damage, Increased Critical Strike Chance, and Increased Attack Speed.
  2. Off-Hand: Shield with Increased Block Chance, Increased Spell Damage, and Increased Health.
  3. Armor: Light Armor with Increased Evasion, Increased Dodge Rating, and Increased Health.
  4. Boots: Light Boots with Increased Movement Speed and Increased Health.
  5. Belt: Belt with Increased Evasion, Increased Health, and Increased Elemental Resistances.
  6. Gloves: Light Gloves with Increased Attack Speed, Increased Physical Damage, and Increased Health.
  7. Amulet: Amulet with Increased Critical Strike Chance, Increased Physical Damage, and Increased Health.
  8. Rings: Two Rings with Increased Critical Strike Chance, Increased Physical Damage, and Increased Health.

Playstyle:

The Bladedancer's Umbral Blades build is focused on mobility and dealing high amounts of physical damage. Use Shift to quickly move around the battlefield and avoid enemy attacks. Use Decoy to distract enemies and create openings for attacks. Use Smoke Bomb to blind enemies and deal damage over time. Use Shuriken to deal ranged damage and apply bleed. Finally, use Umbral Blades as your main attack, dealing high amounts of physical damage to multiple enemies at once.

In terms of gear, prioritize items that increase your physical damage, critical strike chance, evasion, dodge rating, and health. This build is relatively squishy, so be sure to stay mobile and avoid taking too much damage.
